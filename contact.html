Before You Write In

Quick answers

A few things people ask us most often.

How long does delivery take?

Nairobi orders typically arrive within 1–2 business days. Upcountry deliveries take 2–4 business days depending on location.

Can I return an item?

Yes — most items can be returned within 7 days if they don't match the listed condition. Check our Returns page for details.

Do you restock sold-out items?

Every piece is one-of-a-kind, so once it's sold, it's gone. New finds are added weekly, so check back often.

Can I sell or donate items to you?

We're always looking for good pieces. Send us a message with photos and we'll let you know if it's a fit.

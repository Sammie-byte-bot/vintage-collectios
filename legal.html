01 — Privacy Policy

How we use your information

Short version: We collect only what we need to process your orders, improve our service, and keep you updated (if you opt in). We do not sell your data. Ever.

1.1 Who we are

TreasureWave Collections is an e-commerce business based in Nairobi, Kenya, curating pre-loved fashion, electronics, and home goods sourced from Kenyan markets. References to "we", "us", or "our" in this policy refer to TreasureWave Collections.

This policy applies to data collected through treasurewave-collections.vercel.app and any related services we operate.

1.2 What information we collect

  • Account & order data: name, email address, delivery address, phone number, and order history when you make a purchase or create an account.
  • Payment data: we do not store full card details. Payments are processed by third-party providers (M-Pesa, Stripe) who maintain their own security and compliance standards.
  • Communications: messages you send via our contact form, or replies to our emails.
  • Usage data: pages visited, time on site, referring URLs, device type, and browser — collected automatically via cookies and analytics tools.
  • Marketing opt-in: email address if you subscribe to our newsletter.

1.3 How we use your information

Purpose Legal basis
Processing and delivering your orders Contract performance
Sending order confirmations and tracking updates Contract performance
Responding to customer support enquiries Legitimate interests
Sending marketing emails (if you opted in) Consent
Analysing site usage to improve our service Legitimate interests
Fraud prevention and security Legal obligation / Legitimate interests

1.4 Sharing your information

  • Delivery partners: name and address shared with couriers to complete delivery.
  • Payment processors: transaction data shared with M-Pesa / Stripe to process payments.
  • Analytics providers: anonymised usage data shared with tools such as Google Analytics.
  • Legal requirements: we may disclose information if required by Kenyan law or a valid court order.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for their own marketing purposes.

1.5 Data retention

We retain order and account data for 7 years to comply with Kenyan tax and consumer-protection regulations. Marketing data is retained until you unsubscribe. You may request deletion of any data we are not legally obliged to retain.

1.6 Your rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention obligations)
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner of Kenya

1.7 Security

We use HTTPS encryption across our site, restrict access to personal data to authorised personnel only, and use reputable third-party payment processors who maintain PCI-DSS compliance. No internet transmission is 100% secure; we encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

02 — Terms of Service

Our agreement with you

Short version: Shop honestly, and we'll do the same. These terms govern purchases made on our site and set out both your rights and ours.

2.1 Acceptance of terms

By browsing or purchasing from TreasureWave Collections you agree to these Terms of Service. If you do not agree, please do not use our site. We may update these terms from time to time; continued use of the site following any changes constitutes acceptance.

2.2 Products and listings

All items sold by TreasureWave Collections are pre-owned ("treasures" or "second-hand") unless explicitly described otherwise. Condition is described as accurately as possible in each listing, including photographs and written condition notes. Minor wear consistent with normal use is not considered a defect in pre-owned goods unless explicitly described as absent.

We reserve the right to refuse or cancel any order at our discretion, including where a pricing error has occurred. In such cases, you will be notified and fully refunded.

2.3 Pricing and payment

All prices are listed in Kenyan Shillings (KES) and include any applicable taxes. We accept M-Pesa, major debit and credit cards, and other payment methods displayed at checkout. Payment is due in full at the time of purchase.

2.4 Order confirmation

A confirmation email is sent when your order is placed. This does not constitute acceptance of your order — acceptance occurs when your item is dispatched and a shipping confirmation is sent. If we are unable to fulfil your order, we will contact you and issue a full refund within 5 business days.

2.5 Intellectual property

All content on this site — including photography, copy, branding, and design — is the property of TreasureWave Collections or its content suppliers and may not be reproduced, distributed, or used without written permission.

2.6 Limitation of liability

To the fullest extent permitted by Kenyan law, TreasureWave Collections shall not be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential losses arising from use of our site or products. Our maximum liability for any claim related to a purchase shall not exceed the value of that purchase.

2.7 Governing law

These terms are governed by the laws of Kenya. Any disputes shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Nairobi, Kenya.

03 — Cookie Policy

Cookies we use and why

Short version: We use cookies to keep your cart intact, remember your preferences, and understand how people use the site. You can control non-essential cookies at any time.

3.1 What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website. They allow the site to remember information about your visit — such as items in your cart — so you don't have to re-enter it every time.

3.2 The cookies we use

Category Purpose Can you opt out?
Essential Session management, shopping cart, authentication, security tokens No — required for the site to function
Functional Remembering your preferences (currency, language, wishlist) Yes
Analytics Understanding how visitors use the site (Google Analytics — anonymised) Yes
Marketing Retargeting ads and measuring ad performance (if applicable) Yes

3.3 Managing cookies

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Please note that disabling essential cookies will impair core site functionality such as the shopping cart. For analytics and marketing cookies, you can also use tools such as the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.

04 — Returns & Refunds

Something not right? We'll sort it

Short version: You have 14 days to return an item that was significantly misrepresented. Because our pieces are pre-owned and one-of-a-kind, we ask you to review listings carefully before purchasing.

4.1 Return window

You may request a return within 14 calendar days of receiving your order. To initiate a return, contact us at the email below with your order number and the reason for return.

4.2 Eligible returns

  • The item received differs materially from its description or photographs
  • The item arrived damaged in transit (photographic evidence required)
  • The wrong item was sent

4.3 Non-returnable items

  • Change of mind after purchase
  • Minor variations in colour due to photography or screen calibration
  • Wear or patina consistent with the condition described in the listing
  • Items that have been used, washed, or altered after delivery

4.4 Refund process

Once we receive and inspect a returned item, we will notify you of the outcome within 3 business days. Approved refunds are processed to your original payment method within 7–10 business days. Shipping costs for returns are borne by the customer unless the return is due to our error.

05 — Shipping

Getting your treasure to you

5.1 Shipping zones

We currently ship within Kenya only. For international enquiries, contact us directly and we will explore what's possible on a case-by-case basis.

5.2 Dispatch times

Orders are dispatched within 1–3 business days of payment confirmation. You will receive a shipping confirmation with tracking information once your item is collected by our courier partner.

5.3 Estimated delivery

Zone Estimated time Rate
Nairobi CBD & environs 1–2 business days KES 250
Greater Nairobi 2–3 business days KES 350
Major towns (Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, Eldoret) 3–5 business days KES 500
Other regions 4–7 business days KES 600–800

Delivery times are estimates and may vary during public holidays or periods of high demand. We are not liable for delays caused by courier partners once an item has been dispatched.

5.4 Lost or undelivered items

If your item has not arrived within the estimated window, contact us and we will investigate with our courier partner. Claims for lost items must be raised within 30 days of the dispatch date.

06 — Accessibility

Shopping should be open to everyone

TreasureWave Collections is committed to making our website accessible to as many people as possible, including those with visual, auditory, cognitive, or motor disabilities.

6.1 Our commitment

  • Perceivable: content can be seen, heard, or accessed through assistive technologies
  • Operable: all functionality is available via keyboard navigation
  • Understandable: content is written in plain language and behaves predictably
  • Robust: compatible with current assistive technologies and browsers

6.2 Known limitations

We are a small team and acknowledge that some areas of our site may not yet fully meet accessibility standards, particularly some older product images which may lack detailed alt text. We are actively working to address these gaps.

6.3 Contact us about accessibility

If you encounter a barrier on our site or need content in an alternative format, please reach out. We aim to respond to accessibility requests within 5 business days.
